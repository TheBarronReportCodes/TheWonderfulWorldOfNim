Gameplay

Each of the two players take pieces from the pile/piles alternatively. Who goes first is determined by an agreement between the two players or an independent, intermediary party.

What You Do On A Turn

  • Multiple Piles: On your turn, remove any number of pieces from a pile. You can only remove pieces from one pile each turn.
  • One Pile: When playing one pile you must restrict the maximum amount a player can remove per turn, represented by N. On your turn, remove between 1 and the maximum amount allowed (1 < n) per turn
  • You can only remove from a pile/piles. You cannot add to a pile unless playing a different variation of the game. Once a removal from the pile/piles has been confirmed, your turn is ended

Getting Started

  1. Determine the style of play
  2. Determine which player goes first
  3. Take turns (see What You Do On A Turn above)
  4. Repeat step 3 until the condition for the style of play is met (Someone wins/losses)
